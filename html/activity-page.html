Football in Norway

Football is one of Norway's most popular sports to be played with more than 270 000+ active players (numbers from 1. January 2020). With so many active players, would it not be nice to get to know the rules and how to play so you can enjoy the sport even more. Want to expand your knowledge about the rules of football and get to know how to play? Read more about the rules of football and some popular football fields in Oslo down below:

Why chose to play football?

Football is one of the country's biggest sports with plenty of opportunities for new experiences and interactions. Through sport, it becomes possible for you to make friends and memories for life.

How to play football?

Football is played with two teams on the pitch and depending on the age the size of the pitch will differ. When adults play, the pitch size is 120m on the longest end and 90m on the shortest end. You play with 11 outfield players (10 including the goalkeeper) and play a total of 90 minutes (2x 45 minutes).

Suggestions on popular football fields in Oslo

The city of Oslo has a large selection of football pitches that are suitable for both professional players and those who play for fun. Here are four suggestion of football pitches you should visit in Oslo:

Differences between football fields in east end and west end of Oslo

The football picthes in Oslo vary between the different ends in Oslo. In the west, you will find more new and upkept pitches, often linked to wealthier sports teams and better public funding. In the east, there are more people and fewer inferior fields, which especially affects young people who also want to enjoy football.
